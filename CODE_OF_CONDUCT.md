# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at henrychong-ai@gmail.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
